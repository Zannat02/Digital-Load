What is SWOT Analysis?

Business decisions become easier when you clearly understand where your company stands today. SWOT Analysis is a strategic framework used to evaluate your business from four different perspectives, helping you recognize strengths, improve weaknesses, explore opportunities and prepare for potential threats.

FOUR COMPONENTS

The Core of SWOT Analysis

SWOT Analysis is built on four essential components that help businesses evaluate their current position, discover growth opportunities and prepare for future challenges.

Strengths

Identify the internal advantages that give your business a competitive edge. Strong branding, skilled teams, loyal customers and efficient processes help build long-term business success and sustainable growth.

Weaknesses

Discover the internal limitations that may slow business growth. Identifying weaknesses helps organizations improve operations, strengthen performance and make better strategic decisions.

Opportunities

Explore external opportunities that can create new possibilities for business expansion, innovation, partnerships and long-term market growth.

Threats

Identify external risks such as increasing competition, changing market trends, economic uncertainty and technological disruption before they impact your business.
